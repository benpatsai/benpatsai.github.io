Short bio:

I am a Senior Research Scientist at NVIDIA Research. My research focuses on computer system and architecture for emerging applications. I received my S.M. and Ph.D. degrees in CS from MIT, advised by Prof. Daniel Sanchez at MIT CSAIL. My current research projects focus on tensor accelerators design and modeling, memory hierarchy optimizations for domain-specific accelerators, and hardware acceleration for autonomous machines. During my Ph.D., I also worked on memory hierarchy design, resource management in multi-core systems, and software/hardware co-optimization for object-based programming model.

Last update: July 2022
